About Us

Welcome to Denver's most exclusive mobile knife sharping service. We provide white-glove services to ensure your cutlery stays razor-sharp and maintained year-round.

Services

What We Do.

Our process starts with a thorough inspection of all cutlery. We use a microscope to ensure we remove only what is necessary to achieve a razor-sharp blade. This not only prolongs the life of your cutlery but it also ensures your blades sharpening angle never changes.

Sharpening

Razor-sharp guaranteed.

Repairing

Tip, edge, chip and bent blade repair.

Re-profiling

We can taylor your blades to your needs.

Delivery

How It Works

Our team of experts will put your knives under the microscope and let you know exactly what they need. We sharpen, repair, reprofile, and more. Sit back and relax, we'll take it from here.

Services

Are Your Knives Not Cutting It?

Whether you’re cooking, hunting, sculpting, ranching, or simply entertaining friends, you need knives you can trust. Sharp knives aren’t just better for the way your food looks and tastes––they’re safer. We prolong the life of your cutlery, and your fingers, too.

Inspecting

We inspect each blade under the microscope before and after we work to double-check that every nick is straightened out.

Sharpening

We always sharpen by hand to ensure your knife has the right edge angle and that the finished blade is razor-sharp.

Repairing

We fix everything from chipped blades to broken tips so you don’t have to buy new knives.

Reprofiling

We modify the edge, geometry, and bevel of your blades to tailor them to your needs.
